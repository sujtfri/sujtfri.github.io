#### Call for papers: Language Technologies & Digital Humanities Conference 2026

Our research team is part of the organising committee for the biennial conference “Language Technologies and Digital Humanities”.

Start date: 17 September
End date: 18 September
Location: Faculty of Computer and Information Science, University of Ljubljana, Večna pot 113


Important dates:

April 24: submission deadline for papers and extended abstracts
June 12: notification of acceptance
August 7: deadline for final versions
August 28: registration deadline
September 16: pre-conference events and workshops
September 17-18: conference

More information about the conference topics, submission guidelines, student papers, and the organising committee can be found on the conference website.

More information is at this link: https://www.sdjt.si/wp/jtdh-2026-en/
